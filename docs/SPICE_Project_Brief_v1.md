**What Is SPICE?**

SPICE is a crypto-native macro hedge protocol. It is a smart contract
vault that holds a curated basket of hard assets, crisis-resilient
positions, and AI infrastructure exposure, represented by a single
tradeable token called ZPC.

It functions economically like an ETF --- investors deposit Bitcoin,
receive ZPC tokens representing fractional ownership of the vault, and
can redeem at any time for the underlying assets. But unlike an ETF it
is entirely on-chain, entirely crypto-denominated, and has zero fiat
exposure at any point in its structure.

The product is aimed at ordinary investors who want to put approximately
10% of their portfolio into a hedge against systemic financial stress
--- people who sense something is wrong and want a measured, intelligent
way to act on that instinct without betting everything on catastrophe.

**The Macro Thesis**

The thesis is built on a collision between two irresistible and
contradictory forces:

**Force 1 --- AI Deflation**

Artificial intelligence is structurally deflationary. It destroys jobs,
compresses margins, concentrates productivity gains in capital rather
than labour, and reduces the cost of goods and services at scale. Within
3--5 years this impact will be macroeconomically impossible to ignore.

**Force 2 --- Sovereign Debt Trap**

Simultaneously, governments worldwide carry debt loads that can only be
sustainably managed through sustained inflation. The US, UK, Japan, and
most of the Eurozone face interest costs that are approaching the point
of crowding out all other spending. Their only tool is to inflate the
debt away.

**The Collision**

Governments need inflation. AI is preventing it. The resolution of this
contradiction will be the defining financial event of the next decade.
Most likely outcomes involve some combination of: financial repression,
currency debasement, bond market stress, capital controls, and a
fundamental loss of confidence in fiat-denominated assets.

The process follows the classic Hemingway pattern --- gradually, then
all at once. The timeline is estimated at 3--5 years, moving through the
usual cycle of denial, fear, and acceptance. The current moment is the
gradual phase --- the time to prepare.

Crucially, this crisis is expected to be global and simultaneous because
AI does not respect borders. There will be no conventional safe haven
--- which is why a crypto-native structure that exists outside the
traditional financial system is not a technical curiosity but the core
of the product.

**Why Crypto-Native? The Core Argument**

If the thesis is correct, the traditional financial infrastructure that
settles, custodies, and prices conventional assets is itself part of the
problem. Building a crisis hedge on fiat rails is structurally
inconsistent with the thesis.

Key advantages of the crypto-native structure:

-   Zero fiat exposure. No dollar, pound, or euro passes through the
    structure at any point.

-   Capital control resistance. In a scenario where governments restrict
    capital movement --- a core element of the thesis --- a
    non-custodial on-chain vault cannot be frozen or confiscated by any
    single jurisdiction.

-   No legacy infrastructure dependency. No SWIFT, no correspondent
    banking, no custodian risk.

-   Real-time auditability. Every holding is verifiable on-chain at any
    moment.

-   Global accessibility. Any investor anywhere can participate without
    a brokerage account.

-   Self-contained value system. Crypto can own crypto priced in crypto.
    The unit of account itself is outside the fiat system.

**Regulatory Position**

This is the most complex aspect of the project. SPICE tokens almost
certainly satisfy the Howey Test criteria used by the SEC to define
securities --- investment of value, in a common enterprise, with
expectation of profit, derived from others\' efforts. The FCA\'s
collective investment scheme definition maps similarly.

However, the project operates in the same regulatory grey zone as the
majority of functioning DeFi protocols and crypto tokens --- including
well-known tokens like Render, Synthetix, and many others. Regulators
are aware of this grey zone and have not pursued consistent enforcement
against purely on-chain protocols with no identifiable issuer.

Risk mitigation strategies under consideration:

-   Jurisdiction. Incorporate in a crypto-permissive jurisdiction ---
    UAE/Dubai (VARA framework), El Salvador, or Cayman Islands.

-   Geoblocking. Robust technical restrictions on US and UK investor
    access to reduce enforcement exposure.

-   Decentralisation. Design the protocol to minimise the role of any
    identifiable controlling party.

-   No fiat. The absence of fiat on-ramps removes many of the regulatory
    hooks that authorities typically use.

Legal advice from a specialist crypto/securities firm will be required
before launch. This is acknowledged as a cost that needs to be solved
--- potentially through a legal partner who works for token allocation
rather than cash fees.

The macro thesis --- the AI deflation wave colliding with sovereign
debt, gradually then all at once --- is itself compelling content. The
zombie apocalypse framing (if there was a zombie apocalypse, what would
be valuable?) is a memorable and distinctive hook. This content approach
costs nothing and builds credibility organically.

**Target Communities**

-   Macro/gold YouTube channels. Audiences already believe the fiat
    collapse thesis.

-   Crypto YouTube and podcasts. Crypto-native audiences who are already
    comfortable with on-chain products.

-   X/Twitter macro community. Luke Gromen, Real Vision, Raoul Pal
    adjacent communities.

-   Substack macro writers. Long-form macro analysis communities with
    highly engaged, financially sophisticated readers.

**Approach**

Outreach to creators will focus on offering genuine intellectual content
--- detailed thesis briefings, early access, and authentic alignment ---
rather than paid promotion. Authentic coverage from a creator who
genuinely finds the thesis interesting is worth more than any
sponsorship.

**Pre-Launch**

Build the narrative publicly before the product exists. Establish
SPICE\'s intellectual presence, develop the thesis in public, build an
audience. By launch, distribution already exists.

**Workstream Dependencies & Sequencing**

While all six workstreams run in parallel, there are important
dependencies to manage:

-   Narrative clarity feeds every other workstream --- it is the common
    language for legal briefs, investor conversations, creator outreach,
    and technical requirements.

-   Fund structure decisions shape both the legal wrapper and the
    technical architecture --- open vs closed-ended affects smart
    contract design, tokenomics, and regulatory treatment.

-   Legal and regulatory work can begin in parallel once sufficient
    narrative and structure clarity exists to brief a specialist firm.

-   Technology workstream can be documented (requirements, architecture,
    chain selection) before the technical co-founder is in place ---
    removing friction from that onboarding conversation.

-   Marketing begins in parallel with narrative --- the pre-launch
    content presence is itself part of the go-to-market.

**The Technical Co-Founder Milestone**

The target technical co-founder will only engage once the project has
demonstrated meaningful progress. The pre-technical milestone stack ---
narrative sharpened, website live, Cayman entity in place, initial token
sale underway --- is the primary objective of the current phase across
all six workstreams.
