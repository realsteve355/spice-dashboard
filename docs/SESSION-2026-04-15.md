## 1. Stage 3 — On-Chain Companies (completed)

### What was built
- **RegisterCompany.jsx** — now calls `CompanyFactory.deployCompany(name, holders[], stakes[])` instead of the old CompanyRegistry. Stakes in basis points (must sum to 10,000). Parses `CompanyDeployed` event to get the company wallet address, navigates to `/colony/:slug/company/:walletAddress`.
- **Company.jsx** — full rewrite. Detects on-chain companies via `isAddress(companyId)` (wallet address in URL). Reads company state from `CompanyImplementation`: name, secretary, balances, equity table. Shows double-entry accounts journal (Sent/Saved/VDividendPaid events from Colony). PAY card for non-secretary citizens.
- **Dashboard.jsx** — My Companies section reads from `CompanyFactory.getCompaniesOf()`. Active Roles section reads O-tokens from `OToken.tokensOf()`, shows type badges.
- **ColonyPage.jsx** — COMPANIES section lists all companies in the colony, links to company page by wallet address.

### Key bugs fixed
- `augmentedContracts` was overwriting `contracts.json` entries with incomplete localStorage data, causing `cfg.companyFactory = undefined`. Fixed by filtering: only localStorage colonies NOT already in contracts.json are merged.
- `contract.filters.*` / `contract.queryFilter()` throw "unpermitted intrinsics" in MetaMask's LavaMoat-hardened browser environment. Fixed by switching all event queries to raw `rpc.getLogs()` + `ethers.Interface.parseLog()`.
- Base Sepolia public RPC (`sepolia.base.org`) rejects `eth_getLogs` with range > 10,000 blocks. Fixed by switching to `base-sepolia-rpc.publicnode.com` and paginating in 5 × 9,000 block chunks (~25 hours of history).
